Installation

Biometrics Generative Data

The fictional character of Pr. Lafleche invites the audience to input DNA samples and neural activity (EEG) into a machine called Junk Sequencer. Using a large database of existing patents, the machine generates a visual and textual representation of a new futuristic object based on the biometric data.

Authoritarian Science Fiction

The authority figure of the scientific team in white lab coats help people feel insecure. A serie of fake psychological tests achieve turning them into docile guinea pigs eager to provide any information about themselves and their family.

Junk Gallery

At the end of the process, a "Junk honorary credential" featuring is given to each participant as a compensation for their contribution to this incredible pseudo-scientific experience. The 3D-printed shapes of generated objects are form a strange gallery where people can browse random agencements of patents and people.
